# Content, account, and mobile posture

The public Benefits Portal is an established production service rather than a
beta or restricted-audience application.

- Each section was built by a different vendor. Navigation labels, button
  styles, spacing, error patterns, and form layouts vary between sections.
  There is no shared design system or style guide.
- The service is English-only. There is no language selector or documented
  assessment of which languages its users need.
- Pages use terms such as "means-tested quantum", "CDA co-funding tranche",
  "household MOE", and unexplained abbreviations including PCI and HHI.
  Content has not been tested with users or assessed for readability.
- Every page uses the title "Benefits Portal". Pages have no meta descriptions,
  and the public site is not excluded from search indexing.
- After authentication, the header shows only "My account". It does not display
  the signed-in person's name or another account identifier.
- The companion mobile application is owned by the agency and distributed only
  through the Apple App Store and Google Play. Its store listing names the
  developer as "Helpful Vendor Labs Pte Ltd", not the agency.

The portal already has a responsive layout, SearchSG, a consistent contact
channel, the official masthead and footer, and complete WOGAA implementation.
